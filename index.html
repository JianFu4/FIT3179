FROM ACCESS TO IMPACT: GLOBAL SUSTAINABLE ENEGRY TRENDS

From 2000 to 2020

Over these twenty years, the world has witnessed remarkable strides in ensuring energy access for all. As the world has steered towards more sustainable energy practices, the environmental dividends are palpable. A declining trend in carbon dioxide emissions per person is a good sign while it shows the efforts in adopting renewable energy and reducing dependency on fossil fuels.
