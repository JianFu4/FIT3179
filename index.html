Over these twenty years, the world has witnessed remarkable strides in ensuring energy access for all. COMPARE BETWEEN CATEGORY

Over the span of 20 years, the electricity generation landscape has exhibited intriguing trends. Notably, the proportion of electricity derived from fossil fuels has remained relatively stable. At the same time, renewable energy sources have progressively increased their share, gradually encroaching upon the portion previously dominated by nuclear energy.

A comparative assessment across five distinct regions provides further depth into the energy dynamics.A notable observation is the gradual decline in fossil fuel usage within the Americas year by year. In contrast, renewable energy, particularly in Asia, showcases a promising growth trend, reinforcing the global shift towards sustainable energy sources.
